Technical Features: derived directly from historical price and volume

1. OHLC (Open High Low Close)
2. Volume
3. Returns: Shows growth or decline
4. Moving Average (MA, EMA): Average price over the time (10-day or 30-day)
5. RSI (Relative Strength Index)
6. MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence) It is the differences of EMAs
7. ROC (Rate of Change) or momentum
8. Volitality (Standard deviation of returns) it measures uncertainity


Fundamentals:-
1. ESP: Earning Per Share (Profit per share)
2. PE Ratio: Price to Earning Ratio (Valuation measures)
3. PB Ratio: Price to Book Ratio (Asset based valuation)
4. ROE: Return on Equity Ratio(Profitability measure)
5. Revenue Growth (Performance Indicator)
6. Dividend Yield (Returns to investors)
7. Debt to Equity Ratio: Leverage


Macroeconomic and Sentimental Features
1. Market Indices: (NIFTY, SENSEX, etc)
2. Economic factors: like interest rates, inflation, GDP growth
3. Crude oil and Gold prices
4. Exchange Rates (USD/INR)
5. News sentiments
6. Market Inflow outflow
